## The Business Model

### What We Are
BookPondy.com is a **vertically integrated digital marketplace** for bespoke travel experiences. We are NOT a traditional travel agency. We are an *interpretive gateway* that connects:

- **Guests** seeking meaningful, customizable journeys
- **Storytellers** (local guides and cultural interpreters)
- **Property Partners** (villas, heritage homes, eco-lodges)
- **Artisans & Workshop Hosts** (craft masters, wellness practitioners)
- **Mobility Partners** (e-bike fleets, vehicle operators)

### Revenue Model

| Revenue Stream | Description | Commission/Margin |
|----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Experience Bookings** | Curated tours, workshops, immersions | 20-30% of experience fee |
| **Stay Bookings** | Villas, hotels, homestays | 15-20% of room rate |
| **Mobility Services** | E-bike rentals, transfers | 25-35% margin on fleet |
| **Bespoke Itineraries** | Custom multi-day packages | 15-20% service fee |
| **Concierge Services** | Special requests, VIP arrangements | Variable per request |
| **STEAM Camps** | Holiday educational programs | Fixed program pricing |

### Pricing Philosophy
- **Rack Rate Transparency** — All prices published annually, no hidden fees
- **GST Inclusive Display** — Guests see final price upfront
- **Fair Partner Payouts** — Storytellers and vendors paid within 7 days of month-end
- **No Dynamic Surge Pricing** — Seasonal rates, but no algorithmic manipulation
